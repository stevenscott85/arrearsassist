Guarantor failure warning

A guarantor can look strong on paper and still collapse if the setup is sloppy. Your website should train landlords to avoid stupid, expensive mistakes before they happen.

Why this matters

A bad guarantor setup can leave the landlord thinking they have backup when they actually have nothing useful to enforce.

What this module should flag

  • Was the guarantor properly identified?
  • Was there direct communication with the guarantor?
  • Was income and affordability checked?
  • Was home ownership actually verified if relied on?
  • Was the guarantor document executed properly?

Guarantor setup checklist preview

  • Get guarantor ID and keep a copy
  • Keep direct email trail with guarantor, not just tenant
  • Check address, income, and affordability evidence
  • Do not rely on vague assumptions about ownership or assets
  • Use a proper guarantor document process and store the final signed copy
