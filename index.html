FAQ

Common questions

Is this legal advice?

No. It is a guidance and organisation tool to help landlords understand risks, structure records, and track arrears cleanly.

Who is this for?

Private landlords in England dealing with live arrears cases and trying to get organised before things worsen.

Does this version save to an account?

No. This version saves in the browser on the current device only. It is a fast v1, not a finished SaaS.

Why build this first?

Because a useful simple tracker beats a polished fake SaaS that no one actually needs.
