Eviction guidance starts with a clean arrears case

If rent arrears continue, the strongest position usually comes from clean records, written communication, evidence preparation, and a clear timeline.

  • Make sure your arrears record is accurate and up to date
  • Keep written communication with the tenant in one place
  • Log any repair complaints and all access attempts
  • Get your evidence organised before taking bigger steps
